An Introduction to Faith Bambas

Hi! My name is Faith Bambas and I am a student here at BYU. Unlike many of my fellow students, I'm not from the western part of the United States. My hometown is a beautiful spot called Mt. Juliet, Tennessee! In October, I returned home from a mission in the Nauvoo Historic Sites and Salt Lake City Missions. Every member of my immediate family before me also attended BYU, but I will be the first to study in the business school as I am applying to the Experience Design and Management Program this June. I'm excited to learn more valuable skills and lessons as I study here at BYU!


MY INTERESTS


Attending BYU Sporting Events

As a little kid, I was taught to sing the Cougar Fight Song and rep the white and blue on game day. Since then, not much has changed except that I now get to attend the games in person in the ROC section! It's become one of my favorite things to do.

Acrylic and Oil Painting

I began to be interested in painting in elementary school when I took an art class. Since then, I've dabbled in watercolors, acrylic, oils, and pastels. My preferred medium is definitely acrylics, but I stopped doing much painting when I went on the mission. I am revisiting my pandemic hobby now by doing paint-by-numbers!

Traveling and Experiencing The World

My second semester at BYU, I decided to sign up for a study abroad. I landed on a non-major art study abroad to Greece and Italy for the spring term. During those six weeks in Europe, I discovered a sense of curiosity and love of learning about other cultures. Hence, one of the things thtat I love the most is taking trips to new places and exploring the treasures there are to see!
